ThinkPads in General

Thinking about ThinkPads

ThinkPads have been the black laptops ubiquitous at the workplace, coffeeshops, and at schools. Although less known than their counterparts made by Apple, the ThinkPad laptop has been the top choice of mobile personal computing before MacBooks made their debut. Thinkpads were previously made by IBM, but the torch of perfecting these reliable and reliably stunning machines has been handed off to Lenovo.

Why get a ThinkPad?

  • Maintainable - Many ThinkPad parts and documentation are readily available. ThinkPads are known for their ability to be serviced by a user.
  • Upgradable - ThinkPads are easily serviceable, meaning that users can easily replace parts such as adding a larger and faster hard drive or adding RAM.
  • Variety - ThinkPads come in many shapes, sizes, and color too (silver ThinkPads were recently released)! With many ThinkPad series from mobility, 2-in-1, to performance, there's a ThinkPad to suit any need.
